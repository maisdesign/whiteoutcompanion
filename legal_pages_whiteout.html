About Whiteout Companion

Whiteout Companion is an unofficial fan-made tool to support players of Whiteout Survival. It helps track, plan and manage gameplay features like chief gear, charms, buffs and map control.

This project is maintained by a community of passionate players and developers. It is not affiliated with Century Games or Whiteout Survival’s creators.
